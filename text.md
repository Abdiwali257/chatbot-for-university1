SIMAD University was established as a higher education institute in 1999. It became a full-fledged university in 2010-2011. Notable events include the establishment of the Institute of Modern Languages in 2014-2015, the launch of annual conferences on diverse disciplines, and the joining of the Association of International Universities. SIMAD University started offering postgraduate scholarships to School members in 2008-2009 and began publishing the Somali Business Review in 2005-2006. The university offers diplomas in IT, Business, and Accounting, and has established undergraduate faculties, the Center for Postgraduate Studies, and the Institute of Modern Languages. Dr. Hassan Sheikh Mohamud was appointed as the Dean and later became a key figure in the university's development.
